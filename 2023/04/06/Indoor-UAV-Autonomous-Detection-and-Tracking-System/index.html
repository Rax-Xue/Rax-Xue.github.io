Indoor UAV Autonomous Detection and Tracking System

The intelligent autonomous UAS design is carried out in the mission context of cooperative reconnaissance of multiple ground-moving targets in GPS denial environment by performing autonomous positioning, target identification, target tracking and other tasks to achieve accurate identification, continuous tracking and positioning of specific targets.

System Architecture

This system relies on the frame of CodeV aircraft, and uses a switch for networking at the sky side, where the gimbal camera, LiDAR, NUC, flight control, and long-range digital image transmission module interact through the network. On the ground side, the onboard computer can be linked through the image transmission module.

The overall software process is as follows, mainly divided into three modules: detection, control, and positioning.

The detection module is responsible for detecting targets, tracking them, and calculating their pose based on the QR code.

The control module is responsible for controlling the UAV flight, entering different modes at different time periods, and completing corresponding tasks.
