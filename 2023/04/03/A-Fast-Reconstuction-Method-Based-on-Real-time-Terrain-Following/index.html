Terrain Following in AirSim

An algorithm for real-time exploration and reconstruction that does not demand any prior map of the area.

In order to solve the problem of insufficient overlap in reconstruction of oblique photographs, a sparse point cloud generated by SLAM is used to optimize the UAV flight trajectory in large-scale scenes, to ensure that the overlap of photos will not change too drastically due to terrain variations.

This work was completed with my assistance to my senior fellow.

This work was validated only in the AirSim simulation environment.
